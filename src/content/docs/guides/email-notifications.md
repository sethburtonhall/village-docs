Village handles email notifications automatically — you don't need to configure anything to get started. When key events happen, Village sends the right email to the right person.

---

## Emails sent to attendees

### Sign-up confirmation

**When it's sent:** Immediately after an attendee submits your sign-up form.

**What it contains:**

- Confirmation that their registration was received
- The event title, date, and time
- A summary of the form responses they submitted
- A **"Manage my signup"** link they can use to view their registration details or cancel their spot

Every attendee receives this email automatically. There's nothing you need to do.

---

### Event update notification

**When it's sent:** Whenever you save changes to a published event that already has sign-ups.

**What it contains:**

- A notice that the event details have been updated
- The updated event title, date, and time
- A link to the event's public sign-up page

> **Important:** This email is triggered every time you save edits to a published event — including minor changes. If you need to make several edits at once, make all your changes and save only once to send a single notification instead of multiple emails.

## Attendee self-cancellation

Every sign-up confirmation email includes a **"Manage my signup"** link. This gives attendees a way to cancel their own spot without needing to contact you directly.

When an attendee clicks the link:

1. They're taken to a **Manage Your Signup** page
2. The page shows the event details (title, date, and time) and a summary of their form responses
3. They can click **Cancel my spot** to remove themselves from the event

When they cancel:

- Their slot is immediately returned to the event's available count
- If the event was previously full, the sign-up form reopens for new registrations
- The cancellation is reflected in your sign-up table instantly

If an attendee tries to use a manage link that has already been cancelled, the page shows a message indicating the signup was already removed.

---

## What you don't need to do

Village handles all of this automatically:

- You do **not** need to manually confirm sign-ups
- You do **not** need to send confirmation emails yourself
- You do **not** need to manage attendee cancellations — they can do it themselves via the manage link

Your only responsibility is keeping your event details accurate, since edits trigger an update email to all existing sign-ups.

---

## Email sending

All emails are sent by Village on your behalf from a Village email address. During the private beta, the email sender and template styling are managed by Village.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I customize the confirmation email?**
Not yet. Email template customization is on the roadmap for a future update.

**What happens if an attendee doesn't receive their confirmation?**
Ask them to check their spam folder. If they still can't find it, you can locate their sign-up in your event's sign-up table and share the event details with them directly.

**Can I send a custom message to all attendees?**
Not yet. Direct messaging to attendees is a feature planned for a future update. For now, editing the event description and saving will send an update notification to everyone who has signed up.

**Do attendees get notified when I remove them?**
Yes, if you choose to notify them. When you remove a sign-up from your end, you have the option to send (or skip) a notification email, and optionally include a personal note explaining the removal. See [Managing Sign-Ups](/managing-signups) for details.
