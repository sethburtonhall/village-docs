As a part of Village's exclusive private beta program, you get full access to all features as we build the platform.

---

## What private beta means

Private beta is a limited-access phase where we're actively developing and refining Village based on real user feedback. During this phase:

- **Full Organization-level access** — see [Account Plans](/start-here/account-plans) for details
- **Active development** — new features are being added regularly
- **Direct influence** — your feedback shapes what gets built next
- **Priority support** — faster response times and direct access to the development team

---

## Beta benefits

As a private beta user, you receive exclusive benefits that won't be available to future users:

### 50% off paid plans for 3 months

When Village exits beta, you'll transition to a paid plan — at **50% off for your first 3 months**.

- **Organization plan**: $7.50/month instead of $15/month

You'll receive a discount code by email when beta ends. The 3-month discount period starts when you apply it, not when you joined beta.

Private beta users have a direct line to shaping Village's development. When you share feedback:

- **Feature prioritization** — Your requests help us decide what to build next
- **UX improvements** — Your experience informs design decisions
- **Bug fixes** — Your reports help us catch and fix issues quickly
- **New ideas** — Your suggestions often become our best features

## What to expect during beta

### Active development

You'll see the app evolving rapidly:

- New features appearing regularly
- UI improvements and refinements
- Performance optimizations
- Enhanced functionality

### Potential changes

Since we're still building, some things might change:

- Feature layouts may be updated
- New capabilities may be added
- Some workflows may be refined

### Stable core functionality

While we're actively developing, the core features you use today are stable and reliable:

- Create and share events for people to sign up
- Build a custom sign-up form for each event
- See who's signed up in real-time
- Email notifications
- Attendees can cancel their own spot
- Remove attendees and notify them with a message
- Add your organization's logo and name to event pages
- Search and filter your events
- Mark favorite events
- Download a spreadsheet of signups
- Give feedback directly inside the app

## Beta timeline

We don't have a fixed end date for the private beta phase. We'll transition to full production when:

- Core features are polished and stable
- User feedback is consistently positive
- We've built out the planned feature set
- The platform is ready for broader adoption

You'll receive your discount code by email when beta ends. The 3-month period starts when you apply it.

---

## If you choose not to upgrade

If you decide not to upgrade when beta ends, your account will revert to the Free plan. **Your data is never deleted.** All of your events, sign-ups, and form responses remain intact and accessible.

What changes on the Free plan:

- You can have up to **3 published events** at a time — existing published events beyond that limit stay live, but you won't be able to publish new ones until you're under the limit
- Events are capped at **15 slots** for new events — existing events with more slots are unaffected
- Group messaging, realtime dashboard, check-in mode, event duplication, CSV export, and organization branding are no longer active

You can upgrade to an Organization plan at any time to restore full access. Your discount code is valid for 3 months after beta ends.
