As a part of Village's exclusive private beta program, you get special access and benefits as we build the future of group event management.

---

## What private beta means

Private beta is a limited-access phase where we're actively developing and refining Village based on real user feedback. During this phase:

- **Full feature access** — you can use everything that's currently built in the app
- **Active development** — new features are being added regularly
- **Direct influence** — your feedback shapes what gets built next
- **Priority support** — faster response times and direct access to the development team

---

## Beta benefits

As a private beta user, you receive exclusive benefits that won't be available to future users:

### 50% discount for 6 months

When Village moves to paid plans, you'll get **50% off any paid plan for 6 months**. This applies to both Individual and Organization plans.

- **Individual plan**: $3.50/month instead of $7/month
- **Organization plan**: $12.50/month instead of $25/month

This discount will be automatically applied to your account when we transition out of beta. No action needed on your part.

### Lifetime beta badge

Your account will receive a special **"Beta Pioneer"** badge that recognizes you as an early supporter of Village.

## How your feedback helps

Private beta users have a direct line to shaping Village's development. When you share feedback:

- **Feature prioritization** — Your requests help us decide what to build next
- **UX improvements** — Your experience informs design decisions
- **Bug fixes** — Your reports help us catch and fix issues quickly
- **New ideas** — Your suggestions often become our best features

## What to expect during beta

### Active development

You'll see the app evolving rapidly:

- New features appearing regularly
- UI improvements and refinements
- Performance optimizations
- Enhanced functionality

### Potential changes

Since we're still building, some things might change:

- Feature layouts may be updated
- New capabilities may be added
- Some workflows may be refined
- Settings and options may expand

### Stable core functionality

While we're actively developing, the core features you use today are stable and reliable:

- Event creation and management
- Sign-up form builder
- Email notifications
- Event sharing and publishing

## Getting the most from beta

### Share your experience

Use the **feedback button** in the bottom-right corner of the app to:

- Report bugs or unexpected behavior
- Request features or improvements
- Share what you love (or what's confusing)
- Rate your experience

Every message is read by the Village team and directly influences our development priorities.

### Try everything

Test all the features:

- Create different types of events
- Experiment with the form builder
- Test sign-up flows
- Explore all settings and options

The more you use Village, the better insights you can provide.

### Stay connected

Keep an eye on your email for:

- Beta update announcements
- New feature notifications
- Important changes or improvements
- When your discount period begins

---

## Beta timeline

We don't have a fixed end date for the private beta phase. We'll transition to full production when:

- Core features are polished and stable
- User feedback is consistently positive
- We've built out the planned feature set
- The platform is ready for broader adoption

Your 6-month discount period begins when we officially launch out of beta, not when you join.
