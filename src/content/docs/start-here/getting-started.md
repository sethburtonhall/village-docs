## Creating your account

Go to [app.usevillage.app](https://app.usevillage.app) and click **Sign Up**. Enter your email address and create a password. After signing up, you'll be taken directly to your dashboard.

If you already have an account, click **Log In** and enter your credentials.

> **Note:** Village uses a secure third-party authentication system. Your password is never stored directly by Village.

---

## The welcome tour

The first time you log in, Village shows a **Welcome Tour** — a 5-step walkthrough that introduces the main features of the app. You can step through it at your own pace or skip it entirely.

You can replay the tour at any time by clicking the **Help** button in the top navigation and selecting **Take the Tour**.

---

## Your dashboard

The dashboard is your home base. Here's what you'll find:

### Favorite events

The top section shows events you've marked as favorites. Favoriting an event pins it here for quick access — useful for events you check frequently.

### Upcoming events

Below your favorites is a list of your upcoming published events, ordered by date. Each event card shows:

- Event title and category type
- Date and time
- Short description
- Current status

### Sign-up activity *(Organization plan only)*

Organization plan users see two additional widgets on their dashboard:

- **Recent sign-ups** — a live-updating list of the latest registrations across all your events
- **Top events by sign-ups** — a ranked list of your most popular events

---

## Navigating the app

The **sidebar** on the left contains your main navigation:

- **Dashboard** — your home page with event summaries and activity
- **Events** — your full list of events with search and filters
- **Account** — billing and subscription management
- **Settings** — organization branding (Organization plan only)
- **Help** — access the welcome tour, help articles, and feedback form

The **top navigation bar** contains:

- A **Create Event** button to start a new event
- Your **profile avatar** to access account options

---

## The events page

The **Events** page shows all of your events in one place. You can:

- **Search** events by title using the search bar
- **Filter** by status (draft, active, canceled, completed)
- **Sort** by date or other criteria
- **Switch views** between a card grid and a compact list using the view toggle in the top-right of the event list

Each event card shows its title, type, date, and current status. From the card, you can open the event, edit it, or access the actions menu.
